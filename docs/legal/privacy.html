Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the operator of DesignFlow (“we”, “us”) handles information when you visit the DesignFlow website or use the DesignFlow desktop application.

1. Who we are

DesignFlow is operated by the operator of DesignFlow, an individual developer. We do not currently operate a dedicated support email or contact form.

2. Information we collect

We collect very little information:

  • Website visitors. Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may log standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, referring page, and access time.
  • Downloads. When you download an installer from GitHub Releases, GitHub may log download activity and technical metadata.

3. Information we do not collect

The DesignFlow desktop app is local-first. We do not operate user accounts and we do not receive your project files, canvas content, or PRD text. As of the current release, the app does not include analytics or advertising trackers.

4. How the desktop app stores data

DesignFlow stores projects on your device. If you enable MCP integrations (for example Cursor or Claude Desktop), those tools run on your machine under your control. We do not receive data sent between your editor and those third-party services.

5. AI tools and data flows

DesignFlow does not send your projects anywhere by default — everything stays on your device unless you take an action that shares it.

  • If you enable an MCP client (for example Cursor or Claude Desktop), your local app becomes reachable by that tool, and canvas or PRD content you interact with may be sent to that third party's AI models under its own terms and privacy practices.
  • We do not receive, log, or train on that data — it goes directly between your machine and the AI provider you configured.
  • You control which MCP clients are enabled and what context they can access at any time.

6. Third-party services

  • GitHub — website hosting and installer downloads.
  • Google Fonts — typography on this website (may involve your browser contacting Google).
  • Your AI tools — optional MCP clients you configure locally.

Each third party has its own privacy practices.

7. Cookies

We do not use non-essential cookies or advertising cookies on this website. GitHub Pages or your browser may use strictly necessary technical storage for basic site operation.

8. How we use information

We use the limited information described above to operate and secure the website and downloads.

9. Retention

Hosting and download logs are retained according to GitHub’s policies.

10. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding personal information processed by our hosting providers. Because we do not operate accounts or collect app usage data, we generally do not hold personal information beyond what GitHub processes as described above.

11. Children

DesignFlow is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. International users

We are based in the United States. If you access the site or app from other regions, your information may be processed in the United States and other countries where our providers operate.

13. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change when we do. Continued use of the website or app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
